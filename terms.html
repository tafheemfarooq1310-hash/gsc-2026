Global Scholars Championship

Official Legal Framework // April 2026

Article 01

Institutional Coordination

Participation is governed strictly through school-based coordination. All students must be affiliated with a registered educational institution. Individual entries are subject to final telephonic verification with the designated school coordinator.

Article 02

Framework & Participation

A participation fee of 1,000 per student is mandatory, covering assessment materials, applicable meals, and certification. By submitting an enrollment, the student and institution accept all academic rules, progressive elimination models, and disciplinary guidelines established for the Kashmir Circuit.

Article 03

Merit Recognition

Cash prizes and physical awards (including the iPhone 17 Pro Max upgrade) are contingent on registration thresholds as defined in the official prospectus. All decisions made by The Scholars' Tribunal and championship judges are final and binding.
