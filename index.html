The Roadmap

01

Intra-School Elimination

Round 1: The Knowledge Grid

A 45-minute logic and general knowledge assessment. 20 students from each school enter; only the top 10 scoring students move to the writing round.

Round 2: Collaborative Rhetoric

The 10 qualifiers form 5 pairs. They are given a global crisis prompt and must draft a 500-word resolution. The top 5 individual scholars from this phase qualify for the Inter-School Finals.

02

Inter-School Championship

Round 3: Monopoly Strategy

All 50 finalists (5 from each of the 10 schools) compete on 12 boards. This tests resource management and negotiation. Only 25 survive.

Round 4: The Speech Test

The "hot seat" round. Scholars get 2 minutes to prepare a 3-minute speech on a surprise topic. Judges select the top 12 based on delivery and logic.

Round 5: UNO Tactical

A high-speed elimination using UNO card logic. 3 groups of 4 players. The winner from each group enters the Grand Finale.

03

The Grand Finale

The Champion's Choice

The final 3 scholars stand on stage. They must reach a consensus on the final game: **Monopoly (Long Game)** or **UNO (Speed Game)**. One game, one winner, one Global Scholar Champion.
