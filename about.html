About GSC

The Mission.

The Global Scholars Championship (GSC) is Jammu & Kashmir’s premier academic arena, designed to identify and celebrate the next generation of polymaths, strategists, and leaders.

Founded in 2026, our circuit moves beyond traditional rote memorization. We test for the “Scholars' Edge” — the ability to combine logic, collaborative writing, and real-time tactical decision-making.

Our mission is to foster a culture of intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and collaborative learning that empowers students to become confident, capable, and compassionate leaders.
